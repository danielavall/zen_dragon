In the Viking world, egg dragons are mythical beings, embodying ancient gods' essence within their precious shells. These creatures, believed to possess unparalleled strength and wisdom, are revered as guardians of Viking heritage. Each egg holds the promise of a formidable ally, destined to shape the future of their kin.

As newborns, baby dragons captivate with their innocence and curiosity, nurtured under the watchful eyes of their elders. Guided by ancient wisdom, they grow alongside their Viking companions, forging unbreakable bonds that will define their destinies.

In Zen Academy's serene halls, a diverse cast seeks enlightenment. From novices to masters, each journey unfolds amidst the tranquility of the surroundings. Here, amidst the pursuit of inner harmony, characters are molded, destinies are forged, and greatness awaits those who dare to seek it.
